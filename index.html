Our Philosophy
Edge
over
noise.

Most market commentary is noise dressed up as signal. Charts redrawn after the fact. Narratives that explain what already happened. Confidence borrowed from hindsight.

Inflex Trades is built on a different premise. Every market is an information problem. The trader who wins consistently isn't the loudest, the fastest, or the most leveraged — they're the one who knows something the market hasn't priced in yet.

We publish independent research on digital assets, DeFi structures, and prediction markets. We write for traders who want to think clearly, not just trade reactively. No sponsored content. No price targets pulled from thin air. Just rigorous, honest analysis of where the edges actually are.
